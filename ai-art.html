#### AI & Artificial Societies * Some AI research deals with the modeling and creation of artificial societies * The behavior of individuals ("agents") in a society may be simplistic * The observation of the evolution of a society at large is interesting * A simple example/starting point is John Conway's Game of Life

Conway's Game of Life

  • Grid of tiles
  • Each tile represents "agent"
  • Simple rules and neighbor activity decide: is agent off or on?
  • Initially: random state
  • Fascinating patterns can emerge, based on initial state
